Frequently Asked Questions

  • When is the payment due? Payment is due before the start of each term. Late payments may incur additional fees.
  • Can I pay in installments? Yes, we offer an installment plan for tuition fees. Please contact our finance department for more details.
  • Are there any discounts available? Yes, we offer discounts for early registration and for families with multiple children enrolled.
