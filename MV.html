Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To nurture inspired learners and compassionate individuals who are prepared to lead, serve, and transform the world through education, values, and holistic development.

Our Mission

At École Malaïka St Dominic Savio, we are committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and faith-based learning environment that empowers students academically, spiritually, and socially to reach their fullest potential.

École Malaika provides quality education with modern facilities and experienced faculty to nurture tomorrow's leaders.
