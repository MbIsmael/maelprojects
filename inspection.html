Latest Inspection Highlights

Our most recent Ofsted inspection was conducted on 15 March 2023. Key findings included:

"Children are happy, safe and engaged in their learning. Staff foster a nurturing environment where pupils thrive."

Strengths Identified:

  • Warm and supportive relationships with pupils
  • Strong phonics and early reading provision
  • Well-planned, age-appropriate curriculum
  • Inclusive learning environment

Areas for Development:

  • Further challenge for higher-attaining pupils
  • Consistency in assessment for learning
