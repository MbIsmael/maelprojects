Our Curriculum

We follow an internationally recognized curriculum designed to foster creativity, critical thinking, and a love for learning. Our students receive a balanced education that prepares them for both academic success and personal growth.

Nursery School

Our nursery curriculum focuses on building strong foundational skills in language, numeracy, motor skills, and social interaction. Through play-based learning, children develop essential cognitive and emotional skills.

Primary School

The primary school curriculum includes subjects like mathematics, science, languages, social studies, and arts. We emphasize experiential learning, teamwork, and critical thinking to foster an environment of exploration and creativity.

Secondary School

The secondary curriculum provides advanced learning in science, mathematics, languages, humanities, and arts. We also offer a range of elective subjects, including technology, business studies, and environmental sciences, preparing students for higher education.

Key Features of Our Curriculum

  • Internationally recognized syllabus
  • Emphasis on STEM subjects
  • Focus on character development
  • Hands-on learning through projects
  • Highly qualified teachers
  • Diverse extracurricular activities
