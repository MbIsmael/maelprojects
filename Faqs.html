Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the age requirement for admission?

Students must be at least 3 years old for nursery school. Primary-level students are typically aged 6 and above.

2. What documents are needed to apply?
  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school report card (if applicable)
  • Passport-sized photos
  • Parent/guardian ID copy
3. Do you offer transportation services?

Yes, we offer safe and reliable school transportation across key routes. Additional charges apply based on distance.

4. Are scholarships available?

Yes. Scholarships are available based on academic performance and financial need. Check our Scholarships section for more info.
