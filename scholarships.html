Scholarships & Financial Support

We believe in empowering every student. Explore scholarship opportunities tailored to support academic excellence and financial need.

Merit-Based Scholarships

Awarded to students with outstanding academic records and personal achievements.

Need-Based Scholarships

Supporting students from underprivileged backgrounds to access quality education.

Community Scholarships

Offered to students from local communities to uplift future generations.

Application Process

  • Submit your academic transcripts.
  • Write a personal motivation letter.
  • Attach your parent/guardian income certificate (if applicable).
  • Fill out the scholarship form.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be an enrolled student at our school.
  • Demonstrate academic excellence or financial need.
  • Must maintain good conduct and participation.

Scholarships are reviewed on a rolling basis and awarded as per availability. Reach out to our admissions office for personal guidance.
