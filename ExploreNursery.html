Our Nursery Learning Approach

Learning Through Playful Themes

At Ecole Malaika St. Dominic Savio, we use fun, age-appropriate themes to help children explore the world around them and develop essential life skills.

Who We Are

Helping children understand themselves, their emotions, and relationships with others

Where We Are in Place & Time

How We Express Ourselves

Using art, music, and movement to share thoughts and feelings

How The World Works
