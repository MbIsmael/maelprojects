Our Nursery Learning Approach

Building strong foundations through play, exploration, and care

Learning Through Playful Themes

At Ecole Malaika St. Dominic Savio, we use fun, age-appropriate themes to help children explore the world around them and develop essential life skills.

Who We Are

Helping children understand themselves, their emotions, and relationships with others

Where We Are in Place & Time

Exploring our home, school, and neighborhood through stories and activities

How We Express Ourselves

Using art, music, and movement to share thoughts and feelings

How The World Works

Discovering nature, weather, and simple science through hands-on fun

How We Organize Ourselves

Learning about routines, rules, and working together as a class community

Sharing the Planet

Understanding how to care for nature, animals, and each other
